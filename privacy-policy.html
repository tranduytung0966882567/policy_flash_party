Privacy Policy for Flash Party

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Flash Party collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using Flash Party, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Flash Party is designed as a flashlight, strobe, music strobe, and LED banner text application. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The app may process basic device signals locally to provide app features, such as controlling the flashlight, adjusting brightness, showing banner text, and detecting audio beats for music strobe mode. Audio used for music strobe is processed on the device and is not recorded or uploaded by us.

App Permissions

Flash Party may request the following permissions depending on your device and Android version:

  • Camera or flashlight access: used only to control the device flashlight.
  • Microphone access: used only for music strobe mode to detect beats or sound levels.
  • Internet access: may be used to display ads, load app services, or improve app stability.

How We Use Information

We use app permissions and locally processed signals only to provide core app features, including:

  • Turning the flashlight on or off.
  • Changing flashlight brightness where supported by the device.
  • Creating strobe and blinking effects.
  • Syncing flashing behavior with music or sound beats.
  • Displaying custom LED banner text.
  • Maintaining app performance, reliability, and user experience.

Third-Party Services

Flash Party may use third-party services, such as advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or app distribution services. These providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Examples may include Google Play services, Google AdMob, Google Analytics for Firebase, or Firebase Crashlytics if they are enabled in the app.

You can review Google's privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited app or device data with third-party service providers only when necessary to operate, analyze, secure, or monetize the app, and only as described in this policy.

Data Retention and Deletion

Flash Party does not store personal account data on our servers. Locally saved settings, such as app preferences or banner text, remain on your device and can be removed by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Flash Party is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
